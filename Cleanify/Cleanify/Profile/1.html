The cleaning itself was very, very thorough. We hired her for move-out cleaning of our one-bedroom apartment in Lisbon and she did a great job. I wish we hired her when we moved in, as the place is pretty old. However, scheduling a time was quite stressful. Texting the available number is probably your best bet over calling or messaging via WhatsApp. The management kept rescheduling on me. When it rolled around to our set time and day, I asked if she was almost at my place, and she said there was an emergency and that I would need to reschedule. I understand that things come up for people, but she should've notified me proactively instead of me asking 15 minutes past my set appointment time. Also, when I scheduled my appointment, I sent them pictures of my place to get a quote, and they said it'd be one price, and the two ladies ended up charging me another price that was higher, weird considering I already pre-cleaned a bit. Overall, she did a really great job at cleaning itself. But if you're on a strict timeline (like moveout) or on a strict budget, probably not worth it.
